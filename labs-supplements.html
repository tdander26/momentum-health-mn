Fast 8+ hours (water only) No biotin for 3 days Watch for the Evexia payment email

Once your labs are ordered, there are three ways to get the draw done. Here's the short version — the full instructions cover every detail.

At a Labcorp lab — about $8

The most common route; usually 10–30 minutes. Schedule at labcorp.com and choose “routine labwork.” Patients have had good luck at the St. Paul location.

At home — about $70–100

A phlebotomist comes to you. We recommend Getlabs first, with Trinity Care Mobile as a backup.

North Memorial Health Clinic

Only for patients seen by a North Memorial provider in the last 3 years. Bring your printed requisition from Evexia; there may be an $8 draw fee.
