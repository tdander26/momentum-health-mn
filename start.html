Step 2 of 3

Now, let's order your labs.

Quick part! We just need a few details exactly as they appear on your ID so the lab can match you correctly. Then we tee everything up before your visit.

Ordering your labs gets you our comprehensive New Patient Panel, the same starting workup about 99% of our patients do. It covers over 60 biomarkers across your blood count, metabolism, thyroid, inflammation, iron, blood sugar, and more.

See everything that's included
Complete Blood Count (CBC) with Differential + Platelets

A full look at your red and white blood cells and platelets, for a bird's-eye view of your immune system and a check for anemia.

Metabolic Panel

Kidney and liver function, blood sugar, electrolytes, and protein. Can flag blood sugar issues, adrenal stress, or nutrient gaps.

Lipid Panel

Your full cholesterol breakdown. Helps spot metabolic syndrome, thyroid issues, and inflammation.

Thyroid Panel

TSH, T4, T3, Free T3 and T4, Free Thyroxine Index, T3 Uptake, Reverse T3, thyroid antibodies (TPO and thyroglobulin), and TBG. A complete thyroid picture, since the thyroid often shows trouble before other systems do.

Vitamin D + Inflammation

Vitamin D, Homocysteine, and CRP, markers tied to methylation and cardiovascular health.

Anemia Panel

Iron, TIBC, UIBC, iron saturation, and ferritin, for a complete look at your iron status.

Key Extras

Magnesium, Hemoglobin A1C, Insulin, and ANA. A1C and insulin round out your blood-sugar picture, and ANA screens for autoimmune activity.
