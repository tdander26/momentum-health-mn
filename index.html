Our Mission

A Different Kind of Practice

At Momentum Health & Wellness Minnesota, we believe the body is always fighting to find homeostasis. When it can't, there's a reason — and that reason deserves a real answer.

We don't hand out diagnoses and send you on your way. We spend time with you. We dig into your labs, your history, your lifestyle, and your goals — then build a holistic plan designed around you, not a textbook.

Combining functional medicine with chiropractic care and applied kinesiology, our approach addresses the whole person: gut, hormones, neurology, and structure.

What We Treat

Areas of Focus

No matter the complaint, the body is always fighting to find balance. These are the most common areas where we help people reclaim their health.

🔥

Chronic Pain

Persistent joint pain, back pain, headaches, and musculoskeletal dysfunction addressed through structural and functional assessment — not just symptom management.

🌱

Digestion & Gut Health

IBS, leaky gut, bloating, SIBO, food sensitivities, and chronic digestive issues addressed at the root — through diet, microbiome support, and targeted protocols.

🦋

Thyroid Support

Hypothyroid, Hashimoto's, and subclinical thyroid dysfunction — including comprehensive thyroid panels most conventional labs miss entirely.

⚖️

Weight Management

Addressing the hormonal, metabolic, and gut-based factors behind stubborn weight gain — including insulin resistance, dysglycemia, and cortisol patterns.

🔬

Hormone Balance

Male and female hormone optimization — testosterone, estrogen, progesterone, adrenal health, and DHEA — using functional lab analysis and natural interventions.

🌙

Sleep & Mood

Insomnia, anxiety, depression, and fatigue often have underlying biochemical roots. We find them — and address them without defaulting to a prescription pad.

🛡️

Autoimmunity

Hashimoto's, psoriasis, lupus, and other autoimmune conditions managed by identifying triggers, calming immune reactivity, and restoring tolerance naturally.

💡

Unexplained Symptoms

Told your labs are "normal" but you still feel terrible? That's our specialty. We find patterns in standard bloodwork that conventional medicine routinely overlooks.
