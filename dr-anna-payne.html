About Dr. Payne

Rooted in Science.
Driven by Purpose.

Dr. Anna Payne grew up in Detroit Lakes, Minnesota, and pursued a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration at North Dakota State University — including a semester abroad through the National Student Exchange Program at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. It was a combination of academic discipline and a deep curiosity about human health that led her to Life University College of Chiropractic in Marietta, Georgia.

At Life University, Dr. Payne distinguished herself academically — earning a spot on the President's List (3.75+ GPA) for ten terms and the Dean's List for two additional terms. She founded and led the Functional Health Club on campus, attended over a dozen advanced technique seminars, and completed a 100-hour Applied Kinesiology certification series before graduating.

Her clinical training included student internships at Life University's on-campus clinics (CCHOP & CHOP), where she performed new patient exams, developed care plans, and administered chiropractic adjustments and functional kinesiology. She also served as a student intern at Wave of Light Chiropractic in Myrtle Beach, SC — where she expanded her skills with insight scans, digital x-ray, cold laser therapy, and advanced adjusting techniques.

In June 2024, Dr. Payne participated in a mission trip to Sayulita, Mexico with Nikau Women, providing high-volume chiropractic care to underserved rural communities — an experience that deepened her commitment to accessible, meaningful health care.

Dr. Payne is Webster Technique certified through the ICPA (International Chiropractic Pediatric Association), making her well-equipped to support prenatal and pediatric patients. She is excited to bring her training, energy, and passion for root-cause health to the Elk River community as part of the Momentum Health & Wellness team.
